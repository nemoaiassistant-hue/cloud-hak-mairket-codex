FAQ

Common questions

What exactly is an "AI worker"?

An AI worker is a specialised AI agent that handles one specific task — like answering website chats, following up with leads, or responding to Google reviews. Each worker runs 24/7 and is trained on your business data. Think of them as digital employees that never sleep, never take holidays, and cost a fraction of a human hire.

Do I need any technical knowledge?

None. We handle everything — setup, configuration, training, monitoring and maintenance. You just tell us what your business needs, and we build the right AI workers for you. If you can send a WhatsApp message, you can work with your AI team.

How long does setup take?

Most teams are up and running in 7–14 days. We start with a 15-minute call to understand your business, then build and configure each worker. The first two weeks after launch are closely monitored to fine-tune responses.

Will the AI sound robotic or fake?

No. Each worker is trained on your brand voice, tone, policies and FAQs. We programme the exact responses you'd want. The Writer matches your style. The Review Agent sounds professional and human. The Responder is indistinguishable from a well-trained receptionist.

What if a worker gets something wrong?

We monitor all workers continuously. For the first two weeks after launch, we review every interaction and fine-tune as needed. You also get a dashboard to flag any response you don't like — we adjust immediately. Critical tasks (like review responses to negative reviews) can be set to require your approval before publishing.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. No long contracts. Cancel with 30 days' notice. The setup fee covers onboarding and is non-refundable, but there are no exit fees or penalties.

Can I add or remove workers later?

Absolutely. Start with 3 workers on Essential. Add The Writer when you're ready for content. Add The Pipeline Manager when deal volume grows. Each additional worker is £100-150/month on top of your base plan.

What tools does it integrate with?

Your AI workers connect to GoHighLevel (CRM), your website, WhatsApp Business, Google Business Profile, email, calendars and more. If you're already using GHL, setup is even faster — we plug straight in.
