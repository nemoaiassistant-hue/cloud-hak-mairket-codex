FAQ

Common questions

What is an AI worker?
An AI worker is a specialised AI agent trained on your business data, connected to your tools, and set up to handle specific tasks — like answering customer questions, sending follow-up messages, writing content, or managing reviews. Unlike generic chatbots, each worker is purpose-built for one job and works as part of a coordinated team.
How long until my AI team is live?
Most teams are live within 7–14 days. Simpler setups (1–3 workers) can be running in under a week. The timeline depends on how many workers you need and how much business data we need to train on.
Do I need any technical skills?
None. Cloud Hak handles everything — setup, training, connecting your tools, and ongoing monitoring. You just review the weekly reports and tell us if anything needs adjusting.
Can the AI handle negative reviews safely?
Yes. The Review Agent has a manual approval option for critical tasks — including negative review responses. You stay in control of sensitive interactions while the AI handles the routine ones automatically.
How is this different from a chatbot?
A chatbot is one tool. AI Workforce is a coordinated team of 6–8 agents working together. Your Responder captures a lead, the Follow-Up Agent nurtures it, the Writer produces content, the Review Agent manages reputation, the Reporter tracks KPIs, and the Pipeline Manager pushes deals forward — all connected to your CRM.
Can I add or change workers later?
Yes. Start with 3 workers on the Essential plan and scale up as your business grows. Additional workers are £100–£150/month each. You can swap workers out or add custom agents at any time.
