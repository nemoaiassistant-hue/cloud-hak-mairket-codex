Nima's Role & Weekly Plan
20 hours per week — focused on growing the clinic's revenue, streamlining operations, and reducing Dr. Sia's non-clinical workload so he can focus on patients.
📋 Daily CRM — 1 Hour Every Morning

Done first thing every day to keep the patient pipeline moving:

  • Respond to new leads and enquiries within the hour
  • Confirm and manage upcoming appointments
  • Follow up with patients after treatment (satisfaction check)
  • Send rebooking reminders to patients due for a revisit
  • Chase outstanding invoices and payments
  • Update patient records and notes
  • Review the day's appointments and flag anything to Dr. Sia

This alone prevents leads from going cold, reduces no-shows, and keeps cash flow tight.

📞 Patient Journey & Retention

Goal: Turn every happy patient into a returning patient and a referrer.

  • Automated follow-up sequences — after treatment: satisfaction check → review request → rebooking prompt. Currently done manually or not at all
  • Google review collection — with 30+ patients/month and 90% satisfaction, there's a huge opportunity to build a dominant online reputation
  • Patient reactivation — identify patients who haven't been back in 6-12 months and reach out. Low effort, high return
  • Appointment reminders — automated SMS/email reminders to cut no-shows
  • Referral programme — incentivise existing patients to refer friends and family
📈 Lead Generation & Marketing

Goal: Increase organic lead flow from 30-40/month to 60+ by improving online visibility.

  • SEO for airwayclinic.se — target more keywords: sleep apnea treatment Sweden, TMJ treatment, whole-body dentistry, snoring solutions
  • Google Business Profile — full optimisation, regular posts, respond to every review
  • Content & articles — educational blog posts on sleep apnea, dental health, treatment options. Content that ranks on Google and pulls in leads
  • Social media — Instagram/Facebook presence: patient stories, educational content, before/after results
  • Paid ads (phase 2) — Google Ads targeting sleep apnea keywords. With a ~£2,500 case value, the clinic can afford to spend to acquire each patient
💻 Tech & Automation

Goal: Build systems that work 24/7 without anyone having to lift a finger.

  • CRM system — proper patient management in GoHighLevel. Track the full patient lifecycle from first enquiry to repeat treatment
  • Website improvements — online booking, live chatbot for after-hours enquiries, clear treatment pages with pricing
  • AI chatbot — answers common questions ("do you treat sleep apnea?", "how much does it cost?", "where are you located?") and captures leads 24/7
  • Analytics dashboard — track where leads come from, conversion rates, patient lifetime value, busiest months
💰 Finance & Admin

Goal: Clean books, clear cash flow, and Dr. Sia always knows his numbers.

  • Bookkeeping — manage the books directly
  • Patient invoicing & credit control — follow up on unpaid invoices promptly
  • Supplier cost review — negotiate better rates with dental suppliers, lab fees, equipment leases
  • Monthly reporting — simple P&L summary so Dr. Sia can see revenue, costs, and profit at a glance
🤝 Growth & Partnerships

Goal: Build relationships that bring in patients without marketing spend.

  • Cross-referral network — connect with local GPs, ENT specialists, sleep clinics, nutritionists who can refer patients
  • Investor preparation — keep the dental investor relationship warm. Clean financials, clear growth plan, professional presentation
  • Competitor monitoring — track what other sleep apnea and dental clinics in Sweden are doing

🎯 The Bottom Line

The clinic already has 30-40 organic leads per month with a 90% conversion rate and zero marketing. That's massive demand that exists naturally.

Nima's role is to:

  1. Capture more leads — marketing, SEO, ads
  2. Convert better — patient journey, follow-ups, CRM
