What are LSAs?

FAQ

Common questions about Google Local Services Ads

What are Google Local Services Ads?

LSAs are pay-per-lead ads that appear at the very top of Google search — above regular ads, the map pack, and organic results. You get a Google Guaranteed or Google Screened badge. You only pay when someone calls or messages you, not for clicks or impressions.

How much do LSAs cost per lead?

Cost per lead typically ranges from £15-70 depending on your industry and location. You set a weekly budget, and Google charges per lead — not per click. You can dispute invalid leads (spam, wrong numbers) and get credits back.

Which industries can use Google LSAs in the UK?

LSAs are currently available in the UK for trades (plumbers, electricians, locksmiths, HVAC), solicitors, financial advisers, mortgage brokers, estate agents, and accountants. Dental and medical categories are available in the US and expanding to the UK.

What is the Google Guaranteed badge?

The Google Guaranteed badge shows customers that Google has verified your business through background and licence checks. Google backs services booked through LSA with up to £2,000 job guarantee in the UK. It significantly increases click-through rates and trust.

How does Cloud Hak integrate LSAs with our CRM?

We route LSA leads directly into your GoHighLevel CRM via tracking numbers and webhook integration. Every call or message is logged, automatically followed up, and tracked through your sales pipeline. No manual data entry.

Why does response time matter for LSAs?

Google tracks how quickly you respond to LSA leads and uses it as a ranking factor. Faster-responding businesses rank higher. Our AI Voice Receptionist answers calls instantly, 24/7 — giving our clients a ranking advantage that no competitor can match.
