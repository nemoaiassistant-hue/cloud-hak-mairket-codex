What is Google & Social Ads?

166|160|153|

Google & Social Ads are managed paid campaigns that put your business in front of people who are searching, browsing, or ready to return. The aim is real leads and customers, not just clicks and impressions.

167|161|154|

Google Ads works well when people already search for your service. Meta ads on Facebook and Instagram work well for awareness, offers, retargeting, and audiences that need more education before they enquire.

168|162|155|

Cloud Hak manages campaign strategy, ad copy, creative, landing page improvements, conversion tracking, and reporting. Ads also work well with SEO, AEO, and Automation when you want both immediate and long-term growth.

Common questions about Google & Social Ads

How much should I spend on ads?

We recommend starting from £500/month in ad spend plus our management fee. Smaller budgets can work in narrow local markets, but enough data is needed to optimise properly.

208|202|195|
Do you create the ad images?

Yes, we handle ad creative as part of campaign setup. We can create simple graphics, adapt supplied brand assets, and test different messages.

209|203|196|
How do you track results?

We track calls, form submissions, bookings, and revenue where possible. Clicks and impressions are useful diagnostics, but they are not the main measure of success.

210|204|197|
What is the minimum contract?

The minimum contract is usually 3 months. That gives enough time to launch, gather data, optimise campaigns, and judge performance fairly.

211|205|198|
Can I just run Google Ads myself?

Yes, you can run Google Ads yourself, but many businesses waste 30-40% of budget without proper setup and management. Expert management reduces wasted spend and improves tracking.

212|206|199|
What ROI can I expect?

ROI varies by industry, margin, competition, website quality, and follow-up speed. We usually aim for a 3-5x return on ad spend when the offer and sales process support it.
