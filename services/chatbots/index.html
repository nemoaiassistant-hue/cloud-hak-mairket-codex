What is AI Chatbots?

What is AI Chatbots?

AI Chatbots are custom-trained website or WhatsApp assistants that answer customer questions, qualify leads, and book appointments 24/7. They help local businesses respond quickly even when the team is busy or closed.

A chatbot is useful when customers ask the same questions before they buy: prices, availability, locations, services, opening hours, and booking steps. It gives clear answers and collects the right details before a human follows up.

Cloud Hak trains chatbots on your website, FAQs, offers, and business documents. We can connect the bot to Automation and CRM workflows so new enquiries move straight into your pipeline.

FAQ

Common questions about AI Chatbots

How does the AI learn about my business?

We train the AI on your website, FAQs, service pages, offers, and business documents. We also set boundaries so it answers from approved information instead of guessing.

Can it book appointments?

Yes, an AI chatbot can book appointments when connected to your calendar or booking system. It can collect key details first so the booking is useful, not just a name and email.

Does it work on WhatsApp?

Yes, we can set up chatbot flows for WhatsApp as well as your website. WhatsApp is useful for local businesses because many customers prefer quick mobile conversations.

What if it gives a wrong answer?

We monitor answers and retrain the chatbot when needed. Complex or uncertain questions can be routed to you so the AI does not pretend to know something it should not answer.

How much does it cost?

AI chatbot setup starts from £500 plus £50/month for monitoring and support. More advanced booking, WhatsApp, or CRM integrations can increase the setup cost.

Can I see it in action?

Yes, we can build a demo so you can see how the chatbot would answer your real customer questions. A demo is the easiest way to judge tone, accuracy, and usefulness.
