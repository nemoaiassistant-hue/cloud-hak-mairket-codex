What is Private AI Server?

What is Private AI Server?

A Private AI Server is your own dedicated instance of an AI assistant — like ChatGPT, but running entirely on infrastructure you control. No data is sent to OpenAI, Google, or any third party.

It is ideal for businesses handling sensitive information: medical practices, dental clinics, law firms, financial advisors, and any organisation subject to GDPR, HIPAA, or industry-specific data regulations.

Cloud Hak sets up the server, installs the AI model, trains it on your knowledge base (FAQs, treatment guides, pricing, policies), and connects it to your website or internal tools. You get a ChatGPT-quality assistant that never compromises on privacy.

    FAQ

    Common questions about Private AI Server

    Is my data really private?

    Yes. The AI runs on a dedicated server we provision for your business. No data is shared with OpenAI, Google, or any third party. Everything stays on your server.

    Is it GDPR compliant?

    Yes. Because data never leaves your server, there is no third-party data processing. The server is EU-based. This makes GDPR compliance straightforward for medical, dental, legal, and financial businesses.

    Does it speak Swedish?

    Yes. The models we use support Swedish, English, and 20+ other languages natively. You can switch language mid-conversation.

    Can it replace our existing chatbot?

    Yes. It can be embedded as a website chat widget, integrated into your CRM, or used as a standalone dashboard. It replaces your current chatbot with a smarter, private alternative.

    How much does it cost?

    Three tiers: Budget at £297/month, Standard at £497/month, and Pro at £997/month. Setup fees from £497. See our pricing page for details.

    What's the difference between this and ChatGPT?

    ChatGPT sends your data to OpenAI's servers. Private AI Server runs entirely on your own infrastructure. You also get unlimited usage, custom knowledge base training, and no per-message costs.
