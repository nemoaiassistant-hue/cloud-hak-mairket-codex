What is CRM Setup & Management?

What is CRM Setup & Management?

CRM Setup & Management is the setup, customisation, and ongoing management of a customer relationship system tailored to your business. It helps you track every lead, nurture every customer, and never lose track of a conversation.

A CRM replaces scattered spreadsheets, inbox notes, and memory-based follow-up with a clear pipeline. You can see who enquired, what stage they are in, what needs to happen next, and which marketing activity created the opportunity.

Cloud Hak usually recommends GoHighLevel for small businesses because it combines CRM, calendars, forms, email, SMS, pipelines, and reporting. CRM works especially well with Automation, Chatbots, and Voice Agents.

FAQ

Common questions about CRM Setup & Management

What CRM do you recommend?

We recommend GoHighLevel for most small businesses because it replaces multiple tools. It combines CRM, calendars, forms, email, SMS, automations, pipelines, and reporting in one system.

Do you migrate data from my old system?

Yes, we handle data migration from spreadsheets or an existing CRM. We clean and structure the data where possible so the new system starts in a usable state.

Will my team need training?

Yes, team training is included because a CRM only works when people use it consistently. We show your team how to manage leads, update stages, use templates, and read reports.

Can the CRM automate follow-ups?

Yes, the CRM can automate emails, SMS messages, task reminders, and lead routing. We design follow-ups so they feel useful and timely, not robotic.

How much does CRM setup cost?

CRM setup starts from £1,000 plus £100/month for management. Final cost depends on data migration, pipeline complexity, integrations, and the level of ongoing support.

Do I need a CRM if I am a solo business?

If you have more than 10 leads a month, a CRM is usually worth it. It pays for itself by saving time, improving follow-up, and helping you win deals that would otherwise be forgotten.
