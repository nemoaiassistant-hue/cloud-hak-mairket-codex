What is Automation?

What is Automation?

Automation is the process of connecting your business tools so they talk to each other automatically. It removes manual data entry, speeds up follow-up, and stops leads falling through the cracks.

A useful automation might send a website enquiry into your CRM, notify your team, create a task, send a confirmation email, and start a follow-up sequence. The customer gets a faster response and your team gets a cleaner process.

Cloud Hak builds automations with tools such as GoHighLevel, Zapier, Google Workspace, Calendly, Stripe, forms, email, and CRM systems. Automation often supports Chatbots, Voice Agents, Ads, and CRM management.

FAQ

Common questions about Automation

What tools do you integrate?

We integrate GoHighLevel, Google Workspace, Calendly, Stripe, Zapier, website forms, email platforms, and many other tools. We choose the simplest reliable setup for your workflow.

What is GoHighLevel?

GoHighLevel is an all-in-one CRM and marketing platform for small businesses. It can replace several tools by combining pipelines, forms, calendars, email, SMS, automation, and reporting.

Can you automate my email marketing?

Yes, we can automate email sequences, drip campaigns, reminders, and follow-ups. The content and timing are designed around your customer journey, not generic spam.

How long does setup take?

Automation setup usually takes 1-3 weeks depending on complexity. A single workflow is faster, while full system setup takes longer because testing matters.

How much does automation cost?

Automation starts from £500 for a single workflow and £1,500+ for a full system setup. Retainers are available for monitoring, improvements, and support.

What if something breaks?

We can monitor and fix issues as part of a support retainer. Good automation includes alerts, documentation, and testing so problems are found quickly.
