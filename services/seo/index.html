What is SEO?

What is SEO?

SEO is Search Engine Optimisation, the work that helps your business appear when local customers search Google for what you offer. It improves your website, content, technical setup, and local presence so the right people can find you.

For local businesses, SEO is usually about being visible for service searches in your area, not chasing empty traffic. The goal is more calls, bookings, enquiries, map views, and useful website visits.

Cloud Hak combines technical SEO, Google Business Profile optimisation, local content, and AEO where useful. SEO builds long-term visibility, while Ads can support immediate demand.

FAQ

Common questions about SEO

How long does SEO take?

SEO usually takes 3-6 months for meaningful results. Technical fixes can help quickly, but stronger rankings and local visibility build over time.

Do you guarantee page 1?

No ethical SEO provider can guarantee page 1 because Google controls the results. We focus on practical outcomes such as calls, bookings, qualified traffic, and better visibility for searches that matter.

What is the difference between SEO and AEO?

SEO targets search engines such as Google, while AEO targets AI assistants and AI-generated answers. A strong local business usually needs both because customers now search in more than one way.

How much does SEO cost?

Cloud Hak SEO retainers start from £300/month. The right budget depends on your market, competition, website condition, and how quickly you want to grow.

Do I need SEO if I have a new website?

Yes, a new website needs SEO if you want people to find it through search. A website without SEO is like opening a shop in a dark alley because customers cannot easily discover it.

What about Google Ads?

We offer both SEO and Google Ads. SEO is a long-term visibility channel, while Ads can generate immediate traffic and enquiries when campaigns are managed properly.
