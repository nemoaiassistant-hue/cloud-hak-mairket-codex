What is AI Voice Agents?

What is AI Voice Agents?

AI Voice Agents are AI-powered phone answering systems that handle inbound calls, qualify callers, and route them to the right person. They help local businesses avoid missed calls during busy periods, lunch breaks, and after hours.

A voice agent can answer common questions, take messages, collect caller details, book appointments, and transfer urgent calls. It works best when the call flow is designed around your real customer conversations.

Cloud Hak builds voice agents that can connect to CRM and Automation systems, so call notes, outcomes, and follow-up tasks are captured instead of being lost in voicemail.

FAQ

Common questions about AI Voice Agents

Does it sound like a robot?

Modern AI voices are natural and conversational when the call flow is designed properly. We test tone, pacing, and handoff points so callers understand they are being helped quickly.

Can it transfer calls to me?

Yes, an AI voice agent can transfer calls when a human should take over. We can route by service type, urgency, location, or caller answers.

What happens if the caller gets frustrated?

We design graceful handoffs for frustration, confusion, or urgent situations. The AI can apologise, stop the automation, and route the caller to a person or voicemail.

Does it work with my existing phone number?

Yes, we can usually forward your existing number into the AI voice agent. This lets you keep the number customers already know.

How much does it cost?

AI voice agent setup starts from £300 plus usage-based call pricing. Final cost depends on call volume, integrations, routing complexity, and reporting needs.

Can it handle outbound calls too?

Yes, AI voice agents can handle outbound calls for appointment reminders, follow-ups, and simple check-ins. We use outbound calling carefully so it supports customers instead of annoying them.
