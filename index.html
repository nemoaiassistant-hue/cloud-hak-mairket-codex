FAQ

Common questions

How much does a website cost?
Our websites start from £1,500 for a single-page site and £3,000+ for multi-page builds. Every project gets a fixed-price proposal — no hourly billing, no surprises.
How long does it take?
Most projects launch in 2–4 weeks from approval. Simple chatbot and automation setups can be live in under a week.
Do I need to be technical?
Not at all. We handle everything — setup, hosting, updates, and support. You just run your business.
What's an AI chatbot and do I need one?
It's a 24/7 assistant on your website that answers customer questions, qualifies leads, and books appointments — even while you sleep. If you get enquiries through your website, it pays for itself quickly.
What's AEO?
Answer Engine Optimisation. It makes your business show up when people ask ChatGPT, Gemini, or Siri for recommendations. It's the new SEO — and most businesses haven't even heard of it yet.
Do you lock clients into long contracts?
No. Websites are one-time builds with optional maintenance. Chatbots, voice agents, and SEO run month-to-month. We earn your business every month.
