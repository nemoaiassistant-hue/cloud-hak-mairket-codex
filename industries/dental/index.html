The problem

Dental practices leak patients at every stage. Here's where it happens.

You've invested years in clinical skills. But marketing your practice feels like a second job — and you're probably doing it alone. Here's what we see most often.

Missed calls after hours

Patients search for a dentist at 9pm after a filling falls out. If nobody answers, they call the next practice. Every missed call is a patient lost to a competitor.

Empty chairs, booked hours

Your hygiene schedule has gaps. Your associate's chair sits idle on Tuesdays. Meanwhile, you're turning away NHS patients and wondering where the private enquiries went.

No patient recall system

Patients who completed treatment six months ago have heard nothing from you. No check-up reminder. No hygiene recall. They've already found another practice — you just don't know it yet.

Invisible on Google

Someone searches "Invisalign near me" or "emergency dentist [your town]" — and your practice doesn't show up. That patient booked with the practice that did.

No-shows eating your day

A patient no-shows a 45-minute appointment slot. That's revenue gone, and you can't fill it at short notice. No automated reminders, no rebooking workflow.

Few Google reviews

Your competitors have 200+ five-star reviews. You have 23 — and half are from 2019. New patients check reviews before booking. Your score is costing you patients.

How does Cloud Hak help dental practices get more patients?

We build a complete patient acquisition system — Google Ads targeting dental searches in your area, a website that converts visitors into booked appointments, AI chatbots and voice agents that handle enquiries 24/7, SEO so you rank for treatments like teeth whitening and Invisalign, and automated recall campaigns to bring patients back.

Yes. Our AI voice agent answers calls 24/7, recognises dental emergencies vs. routine enquiries, answers common questions about treatments and pricing, and books appointments directly into your calendar. It hands complex cases to your team seamlessly.
