The problem

Patients are searching for your treatment. They can't find you — or they give up before booking.

Medical clinics lose patients at every stage: Google Ads that don't target the right conditions, websites that don't explain treatments clearly, calls that go to voicemail after hours, enquiry forms that get answered days later, and no automated follow-up for patients who showed interest but didn't book.

A 20% no-show rate is normal. A missed after-hours call is normal. A patient who enquires and hears nothing for 3 days is normal. None of this should be acceptable — and with the right system, none of it has to be.

Trusted by healthcare providers across Europe — including Airway Clinic (airwayclinic.se), a specialist sleep apnea clinic in Sweden. We understand the sensitivity of healthcare marketing and the standards your patients expect.

FAQ

Common questions from medical clinics

How does Cloud Hak help medical clinics get more patients?

We build a full patient acquisition system on GoHighLevel — Google Ads targeting condition-specific searches, SEO to rank for medical queries, an AI chatbot that answers patient FAQs and books consultations 24/7, voice AI for after-hours call handling, and CRM automations for consultation reminders, no-show rebooking, and recall campaigns. All GDPR-compliant.

Is your marketing system GDPR-compliant for medical data?

Yes. We design every touchpoint with GDPR and healthcare data sensitivity in mind. Cookie consent, data minimisation, UK-hosted infrastructure, consent-based communication, and audit-ready logging are built into every system we deploy. We work with regulated practices and understand the requirements around patient data.

Can the AI chatbot handle medical triage?

Our AI chatbot is trained on your clinic's services, FAQs, and booking protocols — not on diagnostic criteria. It can answer questions about treatments offered, consultation availability, pricing, location, and referral processes. For clinical triage, it routes inquiries to your clinical team. It does not provide medical advice.

What types of medical clinics do you work with?

We work with private clinics, specialist practices, dental clinics, sleep medicine clinics, dermatology, physiotherapy, aesthetic medicine, and other private healthcare providers. Any clinic that needs a steady flow of new patient consultations benefits from our system.

How does the CRM automation reduce no-shows?

We build automated sequences: SMS consultation reminders at 48 hours and 24 hours before appointments, easy one-tap rescheduling links, post-visit follow-up messages, and recall campaigns for patients due for follow-up consultations. The system also auto-rebooks cancelled slots and sends follow-up sequences to patients who initially enquire but don't book.

How quickly can a medical clinic marketing system be live?

The AI chatbot can be live within 48 hours. A full system including website, Google Ads, SEO, voice AI, chatbot, and CRM automation typically launches in 3-4 weeks. We provide a clear timeline before work starts.
