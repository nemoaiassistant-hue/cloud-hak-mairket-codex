Common questions

What does 'Full Stack' mean?
It means we handle the entire customer journey — from generating leads with ads and SEO, to capturing them with AI chatbots and websites, to nurturing them with automated follow-ups. Most agencies do one piece. We do it all.
What's included in the setup fee?
Full account configuration, website build, chatbot training, ad campaign creation, CRM setup, automation workflows, and a walkthrough call to make sure everything works together.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. All plans are month-to-month with no long-term contract. Just give us 30 days' notice.
Why not just hire an ad agency?
An ad agency drives traffic. But what happens when a lead lands on your site at 11pm? Without AI to capture them, you've paid for a click that disappears. We generate AND convert — that's why our clients see better ROI.
What if I already have a website?
No problem. We can work with your existing site and add the AI, ads, and automation on top. Or we'll rebuild it if it's not converting — that's included in the setup.
How fast can I be live?
Most setups take 2–3 weeks. The chatbot and website can be live within a few days. Full setup including ads and automations within 3 weeks of content approval.
